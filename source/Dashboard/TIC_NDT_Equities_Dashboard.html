Universe by Category
Universe Categories
The Inspection Intel coverage universe (22 companies) is divided into five categories: NDT Services, Global NDT, NDT Adjacent (the Inspection-11 Index), plus Flow Control and Mech. & On-Site Services (the Flow & MOS-11 Index). The doughnut shows each category’s share of total market capitalization.
NDT Services
Pure-play NDT and inspection companies: Mistras Group (MG), Team Inc (TISI), Acuren Group (TIC), Oceaneering International (OII), and Expro Group (XPRO). These firms derive the majority of revenue from non-destructive testing, inspection, or subsea asset integrity.
Global NDT
Large-cap international TIC (Testing, Inspection, Certification) conglomerates: Bureau Veritas (BVI.PA), Intertek Group (ITRK.L), Comet Group (COTN.SW), and SGS SA (SGSN.SW). These operate NDT divisions within broader multi-service platforms spanning certification and regulatory compliance worldwide.
NDT Adjacent
Companies with significant inspection market exposure beyond traditional NDT: Transcat (TRNS) for calibration services and Thermon Group (THR) for process heating and monitoring. These provide complementary products to the core NDT ecosystem.
Flow Control
Valves, actuators, and engineered flow products: Flowserve (FLS), Rotork (ROR.L), IMI plc (IMI.L), Spirax Group (SPX.L), Weir Group (WEIR.L), and Cactus Inc (WHD). Adjacent peer set used as a read-across for industrial capex cycles.
Mech. & On-Site Services
Pipeline, infrastructure, and industrial-services peers: Matrix Service (MTRX), Primoris (PRIM), MasTec (MTZ), Clean Harbors (CLH), and Forum Energy Technologies (FET). Public proxies for the broader on-site services peer set.

Analyst Targets & Recommendations
How Recommendations Work
Each company is covered by multiple Wall Street analysts who issue individual ratings. The Recommendation shown is derived from the mean score across all analysts on a 1-to-5 scale:
Strong Buy: mean ≤ 1.5
Buy: mean 1.5 – 2.5
Hold: mean 2.5 – 3.5
Sell: mean 3.5 – 4.5
Strong Sell: mean > 4.5
Example: If BVI.PA has 17 analysts with a mean score of 1.88, that falls in the Buy range (1.5–2.5). Not all 17 are Buys — some may be Strong Buy, some Hold — but the average produces a Buy consensus.
Price Targets: Low/Mean/High are the range of analyst target prices. Upside % compares the mean target to the current price.
Source: Yahoo Finance analyst data (recommendation_mean, recommendation_key)
